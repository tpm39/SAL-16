
To produce a '*.dat' file for the screen:

1) Get a 128x128 screenshot (which gives a 256x256 '*.png' file) NB: This is what I have to do on my 'MacBook Air' laptop ...

2) Convert from '*.png' to '*.rgb' using:
https://www.freefileconvert.com/image-converter

3) Convert from RGB888 to RGB565 using:
RGB888to565.py

4) When loading into Graphics RAM use:
Binary -> big-endian

5) Save Graphics RAM as a '*.dat' file
